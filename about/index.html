About Lina

Lina is a co-parenting app for parents who share care of a child across two homes. One calm place where both parents have the same information.

Why Lina exists

Lina was created because there was no co-parenting app that felt calm and focused. Most tools were built around conflict or legal processes. We wanted something different — a quiet, structured space for everyday coordination between two parents.

The goal is to reduce the mental load that comes with shared care. When both parents have access to the same schedules, lists, and updates, no one has to carry everything alone.

What Lina focuses on

Lina is built around structured communication and practical tools. Threads keep conversations organized. Shared lists, contacts, and information about the child are always available to both parents. Everything stays in one place.

There are no algorithms, no ads, and no social features. Lina is designed to be used when needed and to stay out of the way when it is not.
