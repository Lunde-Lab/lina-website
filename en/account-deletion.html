Account deletion

How to delete your account

Users can request deletion of their Lina account in one of the following ways.

In the app

  1. Open Lina
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select "Delete account"
  4. Confirm the request

By email

If you cannot access the app, contact us at: support@getlina.app — please include the email address associated with your Lina account.

What happens when you delete your account

When your account is deleted:

  • Your personal account information (such as email address and profile data) is removed
  • Your access to Lina is permanently disabled

Shared content (such as messages, photos, and lists) is not automatically deleted, as it may be shared with another parent. This data remains available to the other connected parent.

Some information may be retained temporarily where required by law or for security purposes, after which it is deleted or anonymised.
